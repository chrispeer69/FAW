03 — The Plan

A Phased Engagement

Each phase delivers standalone value. We prove ROI early, then expand — you are never locked into later phases to benefit from earlier ones.

PHASE 1 Foundation & Lead Capture Stop the leaks. Capture every serious buyer.
1

GoHighLevel CRM Sub-Account — Built & Managed

A dedicated GHL sub-account under our master license — no separate software purchase. A unified inbox (forms, email, SMS, social DMs) flows into one visual pipeline: Inquiry → Qualified → Order Guide Sent → Deposit → Build → Delivery. Every lead logged, tagged, and assigned — nothing falls through the cracks.

2

AI Inquiry & Qualification Agent

Website chat + email auto-responder trained on your models, specs, and pricing structure. Engages instantly, 24/7 — qualifies on budget, timeline, street vs. track use, and powertrain — then books visits and hands hot leads to a human with full context.

3

AI Follow-Up Sequences

Automated, personalized nurture across the long sales cycle — email + SMS — so months-long decisions stay warm without manual chasing. Auto-drafted, human-approved follow-ups after every meeting or quote.

4

Website Credibility Fixes

Resolve all pricing contradictions with one clear "Chassis from $X / Complete build from $Y" structure. Remove staging links, repair broken URLs, wire up a working Order Guide download, replace placeholder images, and fix the TRZ / Series 300 naming.

Phase 1 Outcome

Within weeks: no lost inquiries, instant response, a real pipeline, and a site that earns trust instead of eroding it.

Phase 2 & Phase 3

PHASE 2 Outreach & Visibility Turn your assets into consistent reach.
5

AI Content & YouTube Engine

Claim and brand the channel; establish a real posting cadence. An AI-assisted workflow repurposes existing track footage into a steady stream of Reels, Shorts, and LinkedIn clips — plus build-log content that showcases the craftsmanship and pedigree.

6

AI Phone Attendant

Monitor competitor pricing and specs, surface track-event and club lead opportunities, and assist with parts sourcing — auto-compiling briefs so the team spends time deciding, not digging.

PHASE 3 Engineering & Build Intelligence AI inside the work, not just around it.

Scoped honestly: AI accelerates your engineers — it does not replace them. This is the longest-runway, highest-ceiling phase, pursued once Phases 1–2 are delivering.

  • ›Realistic and valuable now: generative / topology-optimized component design, AI surrogate models to shorten simulation cycles, CAD copilots, and BOM / inventory / build-scheduling forecasting.
  • ›What we will not oversell: "AI designs the car." The car is your craft. AI removes the grind around it.
Why Phase It This Way

Phase 3 is the headline-grabber, but it spends the most and pays back slowest. Phases 1–2 fund and de-risk it.
