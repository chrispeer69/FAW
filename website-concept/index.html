Why Fields Auto Works

A small Ohio shop building cars the way the exotics should be built — light, honest, and relentlessly serviceable.

Supercar builders chase headline numbers with carbon and complexity you can't run on a Tuesday. We went the other way. Every Fields chassis is engineered around weight, balance, and the parts you can actually source — so the car that wows you on the dyno is the same car you trust at lap forty.

The result is a hand-built, street-legal track car with formula-car pull-rod suspension, a modern powertrain of your choosing, and a price that leaves room in the budget for tires, track days, and the next set of tires.

02  How a Build Works

From spec sheet
to track day.

A Fields car is a collaboration. Four clear stages take you from first conversation to your first session on track.

STEP 01

Configure

Pick your model, layout, and powertrain. We walk the full option list and lock a build sheet and price — no surprises.

STEP 02

Reserve

A deposit secures your build slot. You receive a detailed order guide and a target timeline for your chassis.

STEP 03

Build

Our engineers and fabricators hand-assemble your car in Plain City. You get progress updates from the floor throughout.

STEP 04

Track

Take delivery, schedule a factory visit, and get track-side setup support. Then go run laps — and come back for more.

05  About & Team

Engineered in Ohio.
Proven on track.

Founded in 2017 by Rob Fields and based in Plain City outside Columbus, Fields Auto Works designs and manufactures purpose-built sports and track-car chassis packages — with an obsession for performance, reliability, serviceability, safety, and style.

Our team of engineers, designers, and fabricators comes from the sharp end of motorsport — from Can-Am efforts to IndyCar championship-winning programs and IMSA campaigns. That experience shows up in the suspension geometry, the weight targets, and the hundred details most builders skip.

Put that passion and skill to work on your next sports car.
